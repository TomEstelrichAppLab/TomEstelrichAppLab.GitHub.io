PRIVACY POLICY

Our principles

In the business of software development, while other companies might base their entire existence on selling their users' data, we assure you that we will never engage in such activities and we are deeply committed to this decision.

Here we treat data with the utmost seriousness and always strive to avoid accessing your data whenever possible, depending on the nature of the application or service we provide.

Currently, none of our apps access sensitive information. As a result, we are not required to have our own privacy policies. Nevertheless, we believe that privacy is important and should be addressed with our customers. It is never too early to establish a strong foundation for the future.

From the moment we need to access your information to deliver the services we offer, our privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

By using any of our products, you accept the terms of this privacy policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our website or our products.

Changes on the privacy policy

It is our policy to post any changes we make to our privacy policy on this page. Your continued use of our Website or products after we make changes is deemed to be acceptance of those changes, so please check the privacy policy periodically for updates.

Questions and suggestions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or don't see your concerns addressed here, please contact us at feedback@tomestelrich.dev
